What is P35?

P35 is a university building belonging to Oslo Metropolitan University. The building is located in the center of the campus in heart of the Oslo, the capital of Norway. The building homes different faculties and has many facilities for the students such as a canteen, a library, several study rooms and laboratories.

In Oslo Metropolitan University P35 you will find the following:

  1. On the first floor is the canteen, follow the link for more information
  2. On the second floor there are classrooms and group rooms that can be booked
  3. On the third floor is the library, follow the link for more information
  4. On the fourth floor is (datatorget), where you can gather with fellow students
