Canteen in P35

Olaf spiseri

Olaf spiseri is the name of the canteen in P35 at OsloMet university. It is located at P35 on the first floor. The canteen offers a buffet with hot dishes, soups, toppings, and bread where you pay by weight. You will also find a wide selection of wraps and spreads, as well as cakes and various hot and cold beverages.
